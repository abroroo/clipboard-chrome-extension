Privacy Policy for Cliply

Effective Date: December 12, 2024

Introduction

Cliply is a Chrome extension designed to provide a floating clipboard panel for saving, organizing, and copying text snippets directly on any webpage. This Privacy Policy explains how Cliply handles user data and permissions.

Data Collection and Usage

  • Cliply does not collect, store, or share any personal data, browsing activity, or clipboard content with external servers or third parties.
  • All saved text snippets are stored locally in the user's browser using Chrome's storage API. No data leaves the user's device.

Permissions

  • Host Permissions (<all_urls>): Required to enable the floating clipboard panel on any webpage. This permission allows Cliply to interact with webpage content for the purpose of providing its functionality. Cliply does not use this permission to monitor or collect browsing activity.
  • ClipboardWrite Permission: Used to allow users to copy text snippets to the clipboard. Cliply does not monitor or store clipboard data.

Changes to This Policy

Cliply may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Any updates will be reflected in this document with an updated effective date.
